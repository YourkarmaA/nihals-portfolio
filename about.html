About me


I'm Nihal Mohan Reddy, a passionate storyteller whose cinematic journey is deeply rooted in a blend of family heritage and personal inspiration. Hailing from Kurnool, Andra Pradesh, I draw inspiration from my family's military legacy and my father's love for film.

My skills in the realm of cinematography have evolved through hands-on experiences, from mastering natural lighting in freelance projects to integrating social media into my cinematic pursuits. Each frame, for me, is an opportunity to weave a narrative that goes beyond mere visuals, aiming to evoke emotions and resonate with audiences on a profound level.

I am excited to apply my skills to independent filmmaking in the short term, while my long-term aspirations involve making significant contributions to the cinematic landscape. I envision my work not only as a form of artistic expression but also as a conduit for change, inspiration, and emotional resonance within the realm of visual storytelling.

Beyond the lens, my solo travels and culinary explorations add a unique perspective to my cinematic pursuits, enriching my storytelling with diverse influences. Join me on this exhilarating journey where every frame tells a story, and together, we create lasting impacts within the captivating world of cinematography.
